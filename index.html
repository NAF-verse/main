Thanks for looking!

I'm Mohammad Nayem, the guy behind this website. I'm a Bangladeshi who's been traveling the world full time for 2 years.


I started this travel blog in 2019 to document my own national trips, share my photos, and help others learn how to travel and find some really good spots off the beaten path.


All of the writing and pictures on this site come from my own personal experience.


My friend, Flourine,is also from Bangladesh. Now she joins me on these adventures too.

Together, we've done some bucket list hikes, climbed active volcanoes, seen exotic wildlife, and visited some spectacular castles, temples, and monuments around the world .


I've worked with and been featured by BBC Travel, NBC News, Time, and other companies.


I haven’t been everywhere, but it’s on my list. I hope this travel blog can help and inspire you in your own journeys as well!
